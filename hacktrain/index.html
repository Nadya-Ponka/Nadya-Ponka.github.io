Imaguru Hackathon on Train

[ Minsk - Riga ] • [ August 30 - September 1, 2018 ]

For the first time in Belarus.
Starting at Imaguru on August 30th, continuing on a train in Riga, finishing after our arrival in Riga and ending at ///.
The now legendary Hackatrain attracts developers, designers and entrepreneurs from all over the world, all hoping to strike it big, hackathon-style. This isn’t your typical hackathon. Say goodbye to boring venues and hello to travelling around with more than 50 fellow hackers competing across mobility challenges.
